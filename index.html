Why NOTZR?

NOTZR was created with the COVID-19 crisis in mind. We want to promote solidarity between high-school students, and we want to materialize that solidarity in the form of mutual support in the strive towards academic excellence. This is why we keeps our platform completely non-profit and maintain most of our notes free of charge

What do Students Upload?

Class Notes

lesson notes & completed handouts

Study Guides

cheat sheets & unit summaries

Exam Prep

completed review packages

Who is this open to?

Currently, only students from y8-y12 (upper school) at UCC
